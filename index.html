Rank Category

We promise to provide a guide that meets you at your skill level

This is the starting rank for new players. Herald players are still learning the basics of the game, including hero mechanics, item builds, and fundamental strategies.

Players in this rank have a basic understanding of the game and are starting to grasp teamwork and roles. They are often still working on improving their mechanics and decision-making.

Crusader players show a better grasp of game mechanics and team coordination. They begin to understand hero matchups and more advanced strategies but still have room for growth.

Players at the Archon rank have a solid understanding of game fundamentals and can execute basic strategies effectively. They may still struggle with consistency and advanced tactics.

Legend players demonstrate good game knowledge and often have a decent grasp of positioning, map awareness, and teamwork. They are capable of playing multiple roles and heroes effectively.

Ancient players are skilled and understand advanced mechanics, strategies, and hero synergies. They can adapt to various game situations and contribute significantly to team success.

Divine players are highly skilled and have excellent game knowledge, including advanced strategies and in-depth understanding of hero matchups. They can consistently perform well in high-stakes situations.

This is the highest rank in Dota 2, reserved for the top players in the game. Immortal players are elite competitors who demonstrate exceptional skills, strategic understanding, and teamwork. This rank includes the top 0.01% of players and features the leaderboard for professional players.

The Goal

Our primary goal is to create an inclusive and engaging platform that empowers players of all skill levels to enhance their Dota 2 gameplay through comprehensive resources, community support, and expert guidance. We aim to foster a vibrant learning environment where users can access in-depth hero guides, strategic tutorials, and interactive tools, while connecting with fellow enthusiasts to share knowledge and experiences. Ultimately, we aspire to help every player reach their full potential, fostering a love for the game and a commitment to continuous improvement.
